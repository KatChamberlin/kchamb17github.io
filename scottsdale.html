The Delicacy of Scottsdale

Kyoto Scottsdale

A contemporary Japanese restaurant located in the heart of Scottsdale, Kyoto Scottsdale offers a sophisticated dining experience with a focus on fresh sushi, sashimi, and innovative rolls. This eastablishment also provides full hibachi grill service, leading to a fun experience to bring family and friends. The sleek design and upscale ambiance make it an ideal spot for both casual dining and special occasions.

Sugar Bowl

A historic ice cream parlor that has been serving Scottsdale since 1958, Sugar Bowl is known for its nostalgic charm and classic American desserts. The menu features a variety of sundaes, milkshakes, and homemade ice cream flavors, making it a beloved spot for families and dessert lovers alike. With itself pink exterior and vintage decor, Sugar Bowl offers a delightful trip down memory lane.

Craft 64

A modern gastropub that combines craft beer with elevated pub fare, Craft 64 is a popular destination for food and drink enthusiasts in Scottsdale. The extensive beer selection features local and regional brews, while the menu offers a variety of creative dishes made with fresh, the pizzas are the real show stopper. The industrial-chic atmosphere and lively ambiance make it a great spot for socializing and enjoying a casual meal.

Rusty Saloon Spur

A lively bar and grill that captures the spirit of the Old West, Rusty Saloon Spur is a favorite hangout spot in Scottsdale. Known for its hearty American fare, including burgers, steaks, and BBQ, this rustic establishment offers a fun and casual dining experience. With live music, dancing, and a friendly crowd, Rusty Saloon Spur is the perfect place to unwind and enjoy a taste of Western hospitality. The margaritas are a must-try!
